About Us

Zappy is a dynamic and engaging social media platform designed to bring people together. Inspired by the simplicity and connectivity of Twitter, Zappy allows users to share their thoughts, ideas, and moments with a wide audience in a concise and interactive format. Our platform boasts an intuitive user interface to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all.


Founder: Dafydd Evans

"Creating Zappy has been a passion project for me, driven by my desire to gain hands-on experience in web design and development. My goal with Zappy is to not only create a functional and enjoyable platform but also to grow as a web designer. This journey has been incredibly rewarding, and I'm excited to see where it leads me next."
